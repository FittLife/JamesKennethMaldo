Bais City National Science High School (often called Sci-Hi Bais or BCNSHS) is a public science high school Located in Bais City, Negros Oriental, Philippines.


It serves students from Grades 7 to 12, following the K-12 basic education system. BCNSHS is recognized by the Department of Education (DepEd) as a specialized science high school.


BCNSHS aims for academic exceLlence, particularly in science, mathematics, and related STEM areas, while also nurturing students' skills, character, and values. There is an effort to maintain a child-friendly school environment, which includes support structures for students, involvement of families and community stakeholders, and ensuring that Learning objectives are met in a supportive setting.


Science High School Curriculum: BCNSHS uses a modified science high school curriculum (from DECS/DepEd) adapted to align with the K-12 Junior High and Senior High track. This means a stronger emphasis on science, mathematics, research, and technical subjects.


Holistic Development: The school does not focus solely on academics. It encourages involvement in sports, research, arts, and other extracurriculars to help students develop broader skills and values.


Community and Stakeholder Engagement: There's active participation from parents, Local government, and community partners in supporting the school's activities and improvements.
